JHALMURI RECIPE

  • Murhi(Puffed Rice)
  • Mustard Oil
  • MIxture
  • Salt
  • Red chili powder(desi)
  • Indian mango pickle powder
  • Sprouts
  • Onion
  1. Take a high wall utensil
  2. Cut Onion into small pieces(chopped onion)
  3. Put puffed rice into the utensil
  4. First add little oil into it
  5. Then add some salt(chutki bhar)
  6. Then add red chili powder
  7. THen mixture
  8. THen add sprouts
  9. At last add onion
  10. Mix well
  11. Serve it right there
