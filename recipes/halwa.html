SUJI KA HALWA

Ingredients

  • Suji:as much you like
  • Sugar:approx same weight as sugar
  • Ghee:As much you likeüòÅ
  • Water:Enough to make
  • Dry friuts(optional)

Recipe

  1. Turn on the gas and put pan on it
  2. Add Ghee and wait till it heat
  3. Add dry fruits and stir till all gets brown
  4. Take out the dry fruits from the pan
  5. Add more ghee and heat
  6. Add whole suji into the pan
  7. Minimise the heat and stir suji till it gets a golden colour
  8. Add Sugar till it looks somewhat sticky
  9. Add water and put the flame on medium
  10. Water may fly towards you so be prepared
  11. Stir continously and roughly
  12. Stir till almost all water boils
  13. Serve hot
