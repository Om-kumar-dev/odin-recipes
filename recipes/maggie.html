Maggie recipe 10minutes

  • Maggie(1 medium packet)
  • Chopped Onion(1 medium)
  • Tomato(1 small)
  • Garlic(3-4 pieces)
  • Green chili(1 medium)
  • Cauliflower(0.5/4 of 1 medium cauliflower)
  • Mustard Oil
  • Sezwan Chutney
  • Garam masala(1tbsp)
  • seasonal vegetables that can be added
  1. Turn on the gas and put pan on it
  2. Add oil and wait till heat
  3. Add chopped onion
  4. stir for a minute and add garlic and green chili
  5. add cauliflower and tomato
  6. add all vegetable rest
  7. stirr for some minutes
  8. add salt and garam masala
  9. Add Sezwan chutney into it
  10. Stir for some time
  11. Take out the veggies into a container
  12. Add one cup(200ml) of water
  13. Add maggie
  14. Wait for some seconds
  15. Add maggie masala
  16. Wait for few Seconds
  17. Add Cooked Veggies
  18. Let it boil for some minutes
  19. Serve it
